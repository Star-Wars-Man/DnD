High King Borin Stonefist

Title: High King of Thalindor, Lord of Stoneguard

Background: Borin Stonefist hails from the royal line of Thalindor, a family known for their unmatched skill in both battle and craftsmanship. Before ascending to the throne, Borin was a renowned warrior and blacksmith, earning his moniker "Stonefist" for his legendary gauntlets, which he crafted himself. These gauntlets are said to be so strong that they can shatter stone with a single blow. His ascension came after he led Thalindor to victory in a critical battle against invaders, securing his place as a beloved and respected leader.

Personality: Borin is a stern yet fair ruler, deeply committed to the well-being and security of Thalindor. He values loyalty, discipline, and tradition, expecting the same from his subjects. Though he is a man of few words, his presence commands respect. He is known for his strategic mind, and he personally oversees the kingdom's defenses and the production of its finest weapons.

Appearance: Borin is a towering figure with a powerful build, a testament to his years as a warrior. His hair and beard are iron-gray, neatly braided in the Thalindorian style, with a crown forged from the finest metals of the kingdom. He often wears ornate armor, crafted in his own forges, which is both practical and a symbol of his dual mastery of combat and craftsmanship.
