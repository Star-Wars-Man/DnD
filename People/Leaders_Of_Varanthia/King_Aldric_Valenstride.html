King Aldric Valenstride

Title: High King of Varanthia

Background: King Aldric Valenstride is a seasoned warrior and a direct descendant of Sir Galen Stormsword, one of the founding knights of Varanthia. He ascended to the throne after proving his worth in the battlefield, where his strategic brilliance and unmatched combat prowess earned him widespread respect. Known as the "Iron Lion," he has expanded Varanthia's borders and fortified its position as a dominant empire.

Personality: King Aldric is a firm yet just ruler, revered for his honor and commitment to his people. He values strength, discipline, and loyalty above all, and expects the same from his knights and subjects. While he can be ruthless to his enemies, he is also known for his deep sense of duty and fairness.

Appearance: Aldric is a tall, imposing figure with broad shoulders and a commanding presence. His steel-gray eyes and graying hair give him a regal, authoritative look. He often wears ornate armor bearing the crest of Varanthia roaring lion holding a sword.
