Princedom of Frostholm

Ruling House: House Frostbane

Seat: Frostspire Fortress

Princedom of Frostholm House Frostbane rules from Frostspire Fortress in the icy northern mountains. Known for its harsh climate and hardy people, Frostholm excels in cold-based magic and strong defenses.

Fiefdoms: Icewind Keep, Winterhold, Snowpeak.
