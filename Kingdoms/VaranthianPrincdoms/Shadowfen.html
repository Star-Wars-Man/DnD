Princedom of Shadowfen

Ruling House: House Shadowbane

Seat: Duskmire Keep

Princedom of Shadowfen Governed is by House Shadowbane from Duskmire Keep, Shadowfen is a mysterious region to the north. Its dense forests and marshlands are home to secretive knights specializing in espionage and guerilla warfare.

Fiefdoms: Nightshade, Blackwater, Thornwood.
